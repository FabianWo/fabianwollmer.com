About me

Hello Visitor, nice to meet you! My name is Fabian, 24 years old and I'm a Web Developer from Germany. I like building cool projects and trying to up my skills at all times. Apart from programming, I like doing lots of sports, making music, going on nice walks and meeting friends.My Coding journey started in 2019, after reading a random "How to Learn to code in 4 Months" roadmap Blog post. It looked interesting, but obviously sounded too good to be true. Still, I gave programming a shot and starting following the directions. I quickly got interested and it became lots of fun to keep learning and eventually building things on my own. First only some small course projects all the way to an Ecommerce Theme and a Full-stack app.
So now, 18 Months, lots of courses, practice and projects later and here I am as a Web/Frontend Developer.
